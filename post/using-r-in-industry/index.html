Holiday Designs in R and Using R in Industry

Oct 4, 2018 1 min read event, talk, coding-event

Come out to our November 14th event to create your own holiday design in R to send to your friends and families this holiday season to hear a great talk!

This meeting will include a group coding event (we’re making holiday cards! :D ) and a presentation from a Christine Zhang who works as a Data Journalist at the Baltimore Sun and Baltimore Sun Data Visualization team to discuss how she uses R in her jobs and examples of projects she works on!

Visit our Meetup page for more details and to RSVP.

Details on the location:

Date: Wednesday, November 14, 2018

Time: 12:00-1:30pm

Location: Johns Hopkins School of Public Health 615 N. Wolfe Street Baltimore, MD 21205 (Room W3008 – 3rd floor)
